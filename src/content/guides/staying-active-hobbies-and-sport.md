An active retirement is a happier and healthier one, and Pattaya's warm climate makes it easy to keep moving all year. Better still, almost every activity here is also a way to meet people.

## What's on offer

- **Golf** — the region is well known for accessible courses, a big draw for many retirees.
- **Cycling & walking groups** — sociable, flexible, and easy to join.
- **Swimming** — many condos have pools, and the sea at Jomtien is swimmable.
- **Petanque, tennis, gym, yoga** — clubs and facilities are easy to find.
- **Hobbies beyond sport** — photography, music, language, art and more, often with regular meetups.

## Two birds, one stone

The real win is that activity and friendship go together. A weekly cycling group or golf game gives you exercise, structure and a social circle in one. If you're choosing where to put your energy, favour the things that are **regular and group-based**.

## A sensible note on the heat

The climate is a gift, but respect it — especially in your first weeks and in hot season. Hydrate, favour mornings and late afternoons, and ease into strenuous activity. Check with your doctor about exercising in heat if you have any heart or blood-pressure concerns.

## The bottom line

Pick a couple of activities that are both **active and social**, lean on the climate, and respect the heat. It's one of the simplest recipes for thriving here.
