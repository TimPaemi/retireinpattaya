Staying connected — to family back home and to daily life here — is easy and cheap in Pattaya. A little setup in your first week and you're sorted.

## Mobile SIM

A **local prepaid SIM** is inexpensive and easy to get, giving you data, calls and the text codes many local apps and banks require. Once settled, you can move to a monthly plan if it suits you better. Keep your home-country number active too if you need it for banking back home.

## Home internet

Home **fibre** is widely available, fast and cheap by Western standards — great for video calls, streaming and staying in touch. The catch: **quality varies by building and provider**, so before you sign a condo lease, ask the landlord (and neighbours) what's actually installed and how reliable it is.

## For staying close to family

Cheap, fast internet is one of the underrated joys of living here — regular video calls home cost nothing and make a real difference to staying connected. Set up a routine of scheduled calls; it helps everyone.

## The bottom line

Grab a local SIM in week one, and check the real internet situation before committing to a condo. Good connectivity is both a practical need and a genuine antidote to homesickness.
