Let's be clear and calm about safety: for most retirees, day-to-day life in Pattaya is safe and relaxed. Ordinary common sense goes a long way.

The risk we take most seriously — and the reason this is a core pillar — is **targeted scams**. Older newcomers, often lonely and unfamiliar with local norms, are deliberately targeted by romance scams, "guaranteed" investment schemes, property cons and dishonest fixers. Protecting you from these is part of our mission. This hub lays out the red flags plainly, with no judgement, so you can spot trouble early and keep your money and your dignity intact.
