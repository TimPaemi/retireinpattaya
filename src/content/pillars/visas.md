Getting your visa right is the foundation of a calm retirement in Thailand. Get it wrong and you can face stressful border runs, refused extensions, or paying an agent far more than you needed to.

The good news: for most retirees aged 50 and over, there is a well-trodden, legal path. The catch is that the financial requirements, the paperwork, and the way rules are *applied locally* can change — and they're applied at the discretion of Thai immigration officers.

This section walks you through each route plainly: what it costs, who it suits, and the trade-offs. We will always point you to the official source and tell you when a figure needs checking, because nothing here is worth risking your peace of mind on out-of-date information.
